Prologue

My entire adult life, I have been surrounded by applications. I began scouring the early web, and building websites when I was around 12. Fast-forward a decade, I graduated from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst with a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering. I began studying computer science as a hobby in the years after college, and enrolled in several online courses offered through MIT and UC-Berkeley, before being accepted as a student at Southern Methodist University. While studying at SMU, I forged a deep connection to graphics processing and machine learning, and my love for music production fused with an interest in data visualization; and I began building MusicMind, an Augmented Reality Platform for creating, and sharing unique audio-visual music experiences. I strive to build powerful, engaging software, and I am driven by my curiosity in fostering pervasive multimedia experiences.
